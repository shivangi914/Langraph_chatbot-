# About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS product that provides automated video editing tools for content creators. It is built by ServiceHive as part of the Inflx platform, which converts social media conversations into qualified business leads.

# Our Services
- Automated Video Editing: AI-driven tools to edit videos quickly.
- AI Captions: Generate captions automatically for your content.
- 4K Export: High-resolution video exports available on premium plans.
- Lead Generation: Powered by Inflx, helping creators convert engagement into business.

# Supported Platforms
We support automated editing and lead generation for all major social media platforms, including:
- YouTube (Long-form and Shorts)
- Instagram (Reels and Posts)
- TikTok
- LinkedIn (Video content and Lead gen)
- Twitter / X
- Facebook (Watch and Reels)
- Twitch (Stream highlights)
- Snapchat (Spotlight)
- Pinterest (Video pins)
